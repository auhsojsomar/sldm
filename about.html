Vision

A God-Loving Educational Institution envisioned to provide Excellence in Education.

Vocational Technical and Development of man’s potentialities imbued with social consciousness with a missionary spirit, service oriented, professionally and world class citizenry.

Core Values
  1. GOD-LOVING – We uphold to the life and service to mankind of St. Louise De Marillac.
  2. INTEGRITY – We firmly believe on honesty, accountability and fairness in the performance of our task.
  3. EXCELLENCE – We are task as one team to continuously upgrade our resources to produce globally competitive graduates.
Mission

To promote the Vision of the Institution; It aims to

  1. Promote a learning environment that enhances the values of academic excellence responsibility, service and accountability in partnership with stakeholders and the community.
  2. Enhance the core values and potentials of the students so as to equip him with right skills aligned to the requirement of the industry partners.
  3. Provide more equitable access of different government and private sectors thru scholarships for the poor but academically deserving students.
  4. Encourage participation of faculty in continuously upgrading learning materials, facilities and academic accreditation of the institution.

Students & Rooms Regulation
  • Every students will accomplish the Enrollment Form.
  • All students must attend the Orientation.
  • Complete all School Requirements needed.
  • Trainees must finish the Training Duration, otherwise the trainee will pay for training course.
  • Ask permission from the person in-charge if you will get anything in the Tool Room.
  • Please come on time.
Students & Rooms Regulation
  • Wear the required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) when needed.
  • Don’t leave your belongings anywhere, the school will not be liable for lost belongings.
  • NO Bullying, Eating, Smoking & Vandalism inside the classroom.
  • Keep silence.
  • Keep your cellular phone in silent mode while classes are ongoing..
Students & Rooms Regulation
  • Students must wait for the trainer before entering the Laboratory.
  • Inform your Trainer in advance if you will not come on class.
  • Always maintain cleanliness.
  • Throw your garbage properly.
  • Close the door & unplug all the electric circuit when you leave the room.
  • Clean As You Go.
